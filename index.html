Hello again! Let me tell you a couple of things about me.

I have a background in design and construction - weird combination but hey. After several years in the construction industry, the longer I stayed there the more I realised that it wasn't for me (and my back felt much older than it should). Although having this realisation at the tender age of *censored* was great and all, I wasn't really sure what to do next...

So I quit my job and went travelling like any responsible adult would do. One day when I was meant to be admiring the beauty that is Mt Fuji, I found myself distracted, trying to figure out how to find the odd weight of 12 identical stones using balance scales only three times. Solving said riddle led me to the realisation that perhaps I could put these powers to better use.

Fast forward a year, I decided to swap puzzles for Python, and completed an RMIT Introduction to Programming course and the Le Wagon Web Development Bootcamp, I now work there as a Teachers Assistant. Having had no real exposure to the tech industry/community before, as soon as I arrived in this new world I felt like a baby dinosaur spreading their wings - this is where I'm meant to be.

During the week I do my best to be an aspiring web developer, working on freelance projects and looking for my first role as a junior. On weekends I'm a struggling artist - see examples below.

Hopefully this website gives you a small glimpse of my world. If you'd like to see these super powers in real life, share some words of wisdom of your own or just have a chat, feel free to reach out!
